# Link Building Campaign Workflow Guide

Complete guide to building high-quality backlinks through strategic outreach.

## Overview

The Link Building Campaign workflow helps you acquire backlinks through:
1. **Prospect Discovery**: Find link opportunities
2. **Outreach Preparation**: Prepare personalized pitches
3. **Outreach Execution**: Send emails and follow-ups
4. **Campaign Tracking**: Monitor responses and new links

## Phase 1: Prospect Discovery

### Competitor Backlink Analysis
- Analyze competitor backlinks
- Identify high-quality referring domains
- Find content types that get linked

### Content Intersection Analysis
- Find pages linking to competitors but not you
- Identify resource pages listing competitors
- Prioritize by domain authority

### Broken Link Discovery
- Find broken links on relevant pages
- Identify opportunities to replace broken links
- This is a powerful link building tactic

**Tip**: Focus on pages with high domain authority (70+) and relevant content.

## Phase 2: Outreach Preparation

### Contact Discovery
- Extract email addresses from prospect pages
- Find author/editor contact information
- Gather social media profiles

### Personalization Research
- Research prospect's recent content
- Identify topics they're interested in
- Find common ground

### Pitch Template Generation
- AI-generated personalized pitches
- References prospect's recent work
- Explains relevance and value
- Includes clear call-to-action

## Phase 3: Outreach Execution

### Email Template Preparation
- Personalized subject lines
- Customized opening paragraphs
- Value proposition clearly stated
- Soft, non-spammy call-to-action

### Follow-up Sequence Setup
- First follow-up (5-7 days): Gentle reminder
- Second follow-up (10-14 days): Additional value
- Final follow-up (20+ days): Last attempt

### Scheduling
- Best days: Tuesday-Thursday
- Best times: 9-11 AM or 2-4 PM (recipient's timezone)
- Space out emails to avoid spam patterns

## Phase 4: Campaign Tracking

### Response Monitoring
- Track email opens and clicks
- Monitor response rates
- Identify positive responses
- Track follow-up needs

### Backlink Detection
- Weekly checks for new backlinks
- Verify link quality and context
- Track anchor text used
- Monitor link health

## Best Practices

1. **Personalize Every Email**: Generic templates get ignored
2. **Offer Value**: Explain why linking benefits them
3. **Be Patient**: Follow up but don't spam
4. **Track Everything**: Monitor what works
5. **Build Relationships**: Link building is about relationships

## Outreach Email Template

```
Subject: [Personalized] Quick question about [Their Recent Topic]

Hi [Name],

I came across your recent article on [Topic] and found it really insightful, especially [Specific Point].

I recently published a comprehensive guide on [Related Topic] that I think your readers would find valuable. It covers [Key Points] and includes [Unique Value].

Would you consider adding it to your resource list? Here's the link: [URL]

Thanks for your time!

Best,
[Your Name]
```

## Troubleshooting

**Low response rates?**
- Improve personalization
- Offer more value
- Target better prospects

**Emails marked as spam?**
- Warm up your email domain
- Avoid spam trigger words
- Personalize more

**No new backlinks?**
- Follow up more consistently
- Improve pitch quality
- Target easier opportunities first
