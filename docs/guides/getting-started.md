# Getting Started Guide

Welcome to the SEO Platform! This guide will help you get started with creating and optimizing content for search engines and AI platforms.

## Quick Start

1. **Complete Onboarding**
   - Set up your business information
   - Define your SEO goals
   - Choose your experience level (Beginner, Practitioner, or Agency)

2. **Start Your First Workflow**
   - Navigate to the Workflows section
   - Select "Ranking Campaign" for your first campaign
   - Follow the guided steps

3. **Review Results**
   - Check your workflow progress in real-time
   - Review generated content and recommendations
   - Track your rankings over time

## Key Features

### For Beginners
- **Guided Workflows**: Step-by-step instructions for every action
- **Jargon Tooltips**: Hover over any SEO term to see explanations
- **Simple Interface**: Clean, easy-to-understand UI

### For Practitioners
- **Advanced Workflows**: Complete ranking campaigns, link building, technical audits
- **Competitor Analysis**: Deep insights into competitor strategies
- **Performance Tracking**: Monitor your SEO improvements

### For Agencies
- **Multi-Client Management**: Manage multiple clients in one dashboard
- **Team Collaboration**: Work together with your team
- **White-Label Options**: Customize the platform for your brand

## Common Workflows

### Ranking Campaign
End-to-end workflow to rank content:
1. Keyword discovery and expansion
2. Competitor research
3. Content creation with EEAT optimization
4. Publishing preparation
5. Rank tracking

### Link Building Campaign
Build high-quality backlinks:
1. Prospect discovery
2. Outreach preparation
3. Email execution
4. Campaign tracking

### Technical SEO Audit
Identify and fix technical issues:
1. Site crawling
2. Issue detection
3. Action plan generation
4. Asset generation (robots.txt, sitemaps)
5. Monitoring setup

## Tips for Success

- **Start Small**: Begin with one workflow and master it before moving to others
- **Use Tooltips**: Don't hesitate to hover over terms you don't understand
- **Review Results**: Always review generated content before publishing
- **Track Progress**: Monitor your rankings and adjust strategies accordingly

## Need Help?

- Use the in-app help system (click the ? icon)
- Check workflow-specific guides
- Contact support if you encounter issues
