# Ranking Campaign Workflow Guide

Complete guide to running a successful ranking campaign from keyword discovery to tracking.

## Overview

The Ranking Campaign workflow helps you create and optimize content that ranks well in search engines. It includes 5 phases:

1. **Discovery**: Find keywords and opportunities
2. **Research**: Analyze competitors and SERP results
3. **Content Creation**: Generate optimized content
4. **Publishing**: Prepare for publication
5. **Tracking**: Monitor rankings

## Phase 1: Discovery

### Keyword Seed Expansion
- Enter your primary keyword
- The system expands it into related keywords
- Review keyword suggestions and select the best ones

### Competition Analysis
- See who's ranking for your keywords
- Identify competitor strengths and weaknesses
- Find content gaps

### Opportunity Scoring
- Keywords are scored by:
  - Search volume
  - Keyword difficulty
  - Competition level
  - Relevance to your domain

**Tip**: Focus on keywords with high opportunity scores (70+).

## Phase 2: Research

### SERP Analysis
- Review current search results
- Identify content formats that rank
- Note SERP features (featured snippets, PAA, etc.)

### Competitor Content Analysis
- Analyze top-ranking content
- Identify content depth and structure
- Find unique angles

### Content Gap Analysis
- Compare your content to competitors
- Identify missing topics
- Prioritize content opportunities

## Phase 3: Content Creation

### Content Brief Generation
- AI-generated brief based on research
- Includes outline, key points, and EEAT signals
- Review and customize as needed

### Draft Creation
- Content is generated using RAG orchestrator
- Includes target keywords naturally
- Demonstrates expertise and authority

### EEAT Optimization
- Content is reviewed for:
  - **Expertise**: Demonstrates knowledge
  - **Experience**: Shows first-hand experience
  - **Authoritativeness**: Includes citations and credentials
  - **Trustworthiness**: Accurate and transparent

## Phase 4: Publishing

### Schema Markup Generation
- Article schema with author and publisher info
- FAQPage schema if applicable
- BreadcrumbList schema

### Image Generation
- Hero image (16:9)
- Section images for major headings
- Infographics for statistics
- Social media variants

### Meta Tags Preparation
- SEO-optimized title tag (50-60 chars)
- Meta description (150-160 chars)
- Open Graph tags
- Twitter Card tags

## Phase 5: Tracking

### Initial Rank Snapshot
- Baseline rankings captured
- Current keyword positions recorded
- Used to measure improvement

### Monitoring Setup
- Weekly rank checks
- Content performance tracking
- Content decay detection
- Alert thresholds

## Best Practices

1. **Choose the Right Keywords**: Focus on keywords with good opportunity scores
2. **Research Thoroughly**: Don't skip competitor analysis
3. **Optimize for EEAT**: Ensure your content demonstrates expertise
4. **Track Progress**: Monitor rankings weekly
5. **Update Content**: Refresh content when rankings drop

## Troubleshooting

**Workflow fails at keyword expansion?**
- Check your DataForSEO API credentials
- Verify you have sufficient API credits

**Content generation fails?**
- Ensure you have sufficient AI credits
- Check your content brief is complete

**Rankings not improving?**
- Review competitor content - you may need to go deeper
- Check technical SEO issues
- Consider link building campaign
