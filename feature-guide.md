## AI Content Generation

Create SEO-optimized, EEAT-compliant content using a sophisticated multi-agent pipeline.

### RAG Writer Orchestrator

The content generation system uses a Research-Augmented Generation (RAG) approach:

1. **Deep Research Phase**: 
   - Web research using Perplexity AI
   - Competitor content analysis
   - SERP analysis for target keywords

2. **Content Generation**:
   - AI-powered writing with Gemini 3.0
   - Style matching to your brand voice
   - SEO optimization built-in

3. **Quality Assurance Loop**:
   - DataForSEO scoring for SEO compliance
   - EEAT evaluation for quality signals
   - Frase optimization scoring
   - AEO compliance checking
   - Iterative refinement until quality thresholds met

### Content Types Supported

- Blog posts and articles
- Landing pages
- Product descriptions
- Social media content
- Meta titles and descriptions
- FAQ sections
- Schema markup

### Quality Scoring

Content is scored on multiple dimensions:
- **DataForSEO Score**: Technical SEO optimization
- **EEAT Score**: Expertise and trustworthiness
- **Depth Score**: Content comprehensiveness
- **Factual Score**: Accuracy and citations
- **Frase Score**: Topic coverage optimization
- **AEO Score**: AI citability compliance

### Image Generation

- AI-powered image creation for content
- Optimized alt text generation
- Multiple image styles and formats

## Proactive Suggestions Engine

The platform anticipates your needs and suggests next steps.

### How It Works

1. Analyzes your current progress and completed tasks
2. Considers your business context and goals
3. Reviews conversation history for context
4. Generates three tailored suggestions:
   - **Deep Dive**: Continue exploring current topic
   - **Adjacent**: Related opportunity you might miss
   - **Execution**: Actionable next step

### Features

- **Session Memory**: Remembers details across conversations
- **Roadmap Tracking**: Tracks progress through SEO pillars
- **Personalization**: Suggestions adapt to your expertise level
- **Task Detection**: Automatically detects completed tasks
